A.S.T.R.O.COM.

Advanced Solar Tracking and Rover Observation Communication

Our project is an extension of FSI's RE-RASSOR rover project.
The addition of a reflector tower and a mobile communication tower will allow for rovers to explore
permanently shaded craters on the moon's surface and communicate images and text to each other.
