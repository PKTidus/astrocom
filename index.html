A.S.T.R.O.S.C.O.P.E.

Advanced Solar Tracking and Rover Observation Streamlined
with Communication for Optimized Planetary Exploration

Our project is an extension of FSI's RE-RASSOR rover project.
The addition of a reflector tower and a communication tower will allow for rovers to explore permanently shaded crater on the moon's surface.
