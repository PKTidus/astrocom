Welcome to Group 14's Project Website!

Advanced Solar Tracking and
Rover Observation Communication

Our project is an extension of FSI's RE-RASSOR rover project.
The addition of a reflector tower and a mobile communication tower
will allow for rovers to explore permanently shaded craters
on the moon's surface and communicate with each other.
